🔗 Combined Admission Analysis — Portal Records Cross-Matched with EXTRAEDGE Leads
Every confirmed admission from Admission1 + Admission2 (Digital Campus / NeverSkip Portal) is matched against the Touched Leads sheet using primary mobile, secondary mobile, WhatsApp number, email ID, father's/mother's contact & email. This reveals: admissions present in the Portal but missing from Leads (no EXTRAEDGE lead record), admissions present in Leads (Stage = 08-Admission Completed) but missing from the Portal (not yet synced), and admissions confirmed in both — for which we can identify the Lead Owner / Counsellor.

⚠️ Data Quality — Process Gap Analysis
Process: every admission must originate as a LeadId in EXTRAEDGE, progress to Stage 08-Admission Completed, then be uploaded to Digital Campus / NeverSkip Portal. Two gap types are tracked below: (1) "Not Uploaded to Portal" — Lead Owner marked the admission complete in EXTRAEDGE but the student record doesn't exist in the Portal yet; (2) "No LeadId Found" — student is enrolled in the Portal but no matching EXTRAEDGE lead (by phone, email, or name+branch) was found, so the Lead Owner is unidentifiable.
